About

Locus Soundsystem is a DJ collective in Göteborg, Sweden.

Locus was founded in 1999 by a bunch of good friends, and this makes them the longest running concept for deep electronic music in Scandinavia.

Locus hosts their annual club night at Pustervik since 2001, but they are also throwing parties and playing gigs all over Sweden and the world.

Locus has shared some wild times playing alongside such artists as: Alex Arnout, Andre Kronert, Canson, Click, Daniel Becker, Daniel Czerner, David Dorad, David Keno, Demir & Seymen, Dorisburg, Eddie Richards, Frank Beckers, James Dexter, Kaiserdisco, Madmotormiguel, Marcus Meinhardt, Martinez, Markus Homm, Mira, Mihai Popoviciu, Ray Okpara, Red Axes, Rotzige Beats, Smokescreen Soundsystem, SuperFlu, Till Von Sein and Yapacc to name a few.

We've also had the pleasure to play in places like; Garbicz Festival, Fusion, 3000grad, Secret Island Nation, Czechtek, Sehr Schön, Mythos, Rosis, Renate, The Villa, Gagnef, Arena, Moog, Roxy Prague, New Asia, Future Drome, Sommeroya, Vega, Jaz, Berns, Trädgården, and so on.
